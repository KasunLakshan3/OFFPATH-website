The Travel Dilemma

Why Go OFFPATH?

Sri Lanka has hundreds of breathtaking hidden gems that tourist blogs ignore, leading to overcrowding in a few spots while local communities miss out.

The Overcrowded Trail

  • Overcrowding at major, commercialized locations.
  • Lack of authentic, deep cultural interactions.
  • Unverified blogs leading to safety hazards.
  • Tourism revenues bypass rural local families.

The OFFPATH Way

  • Uncover pristine waterfalls & viewpoints.
  • Learn local lore directly from student guides.
  • GPS-validated coordinates & real-time updates.
  • Empower local communities and fund education.
